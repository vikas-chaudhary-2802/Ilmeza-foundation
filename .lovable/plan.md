

# Ilmeza Foundation — Premium Non-Profit Website

A ultra-clean, emotionally powerful, and trustworthy website for Ilmeza Foundation, built as a pure frontend experience with premium design sensibilities.

---

## Design System

- **Colors**: Deep Navy Blue, Forest Green, White backgrounds, Soft Gold accents
- **Typography**: Playfair Display for headings (elegant serif), Inter for body text (clean sans-serif)
- **Style**: Generous white space, soft shadows, subtle fade-in animations, rounded corners
- **Logo**: Prominently displayed Ilmeza Foundation logo (provided)

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Homepage — Emotional & Premium
- **Hero Section**: Full-width with subtle gradient overlay, headline "Building Hope. Creating Futures.", subheadline, two CTAs (gold "Donate Now" + outline "Explore Our Work")
- **Who We Are**: Two-column layout with brand story text and placeholder impact image
- **Focus Areas**: 6 elegant icon cards (Education, Youth Skills, Women Empowerment, Healthcare, Community Development, Disaster Relief) with subtle hover lift animations
- **Impact Numbers**: Large serif counters with animated count-up (Students Supported, Women Empowered, Youth Skilled, Communities Impacted, Volunteers Engaged)
- **"Why We Exist"**: Centered emotional statement in large serif typography — the premium differentiator
- **Call to Action**: "Together, We Can Transform Lives" with Volunteer, Donate, Partner buttons

### 2. About Page — Storytelling Layout
- Our Story with large section headers and generous spacing
- Vision & Mission statements in elegant blocks
- Core Values displayed as minimal cards
- "Why Ilmeza Foundation" section addressing the problems being solved
- Soft background color blocks to separate sections

### 3. Programs Page — Calm & Trust-Driven
- 6 program sections in alternating left-right layouts
- Each with icon, title, bullet points, and goal statement
- Programs: Education, Youth Skills, Women Empowerment, Healthcare, Community Development, Disaster Relief

### 4. Impact & Stories Page
- Impact summary with key achievements
- Testimonial cards with quotes in large serif font
- Placeholder sections for success stories and before/after journeys

### 5. Get Involved Page
- Three large invitation-style blocks: Volunteer, Partner, Donate
- Each with description, opportunities list, and CTA button
- Warm, inviting tone — not salesy

### 6. Legal & Transparency Page
- Formal trust section with Section 8 registration, 12A & 80G certification, CSR registration details
- Annual reports download section (placeholder links)
- Financial transparency statement
- Clean, authoritative design

### 7. Contact Page
- Contact information (address, phone, email, website)
- Clean contact form (Name, Email, Subject, Message)
- Embedded Google Maps placeholder

---

## Shared Components
- **Navigation**: Minimal sticky header with large logo, clean nav links, gold "Donate Now" button
- **Footer**: Minimalist with foundation info, quick links, social media icons (monochrome), contact details
- **Smooth scroll & fade-in transitions** on all sections
- **Mobile-first responsive design** throughout

---

## Key Premium Touches
- Soft gold hover effects on buttons
- Subtle fade-in animations as sections enter viewport
- Elegant divider lines between sections
- Soft shadow layering on cards
- Monochrome social media icons
- No clutter, no popups, no aggressive elements
